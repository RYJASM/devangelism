Erika Heidi

PHP Evangelist

Erika Heidi is a web developer / devOp working with PHP and Linux for over a decade. Author of the Vagrant Cookbook and open source projects like phansible.com and dev-human.org, she worked many years as an independent developer, experimenting with a large diversity of projects and tools. Erika is an active open source contributor, speaker and writer.

Talks

  • Portable Environments with Vagrant, Laracon EU 2014
  • Vagrant Provisioners in a Nutshell, PhpDay Italy 2014

Annie Dane

Director of Production & Events

Director of Production & Events , Annie Dane, has been producing events for 10 years, her passion for the startup and developer community began in 2013 when she began hosting hundreds of events at WeWork. In March 2014, Annie decided to move her focus to the developer community, joining the amazing and loving DigitalOcean family.

Her passion is focused on event production, project management and producing developer events that inspire developers to get out of their seats and meet other developers.

Annie is looking forward to supporting the developer community through DigitalOcean’s surprise and delight initiatives, meetups, conferences, hangouts and workshops.
